Lasagna Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 pound ground beef or sausage (optional)
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. If using meat, brown it in a skillet over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  5. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, meat (if using), marinara sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
