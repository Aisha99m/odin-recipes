Pasta Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes (with their juice) and dried basil. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to combine, ensuring the pasta is well coated with the sauce.
  5. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Enjoy your delicious pasta!
